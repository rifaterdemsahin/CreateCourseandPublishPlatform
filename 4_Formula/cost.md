Skilljar pricing isn’t simple or public like many SaaS tools—it’s **quote-based and aimed at mid–large companies**, so costs vary a lot. But we do have reliable ranges:

---

## 💰 Typical Skilljar cost (realistic ranges)

### 📊 Entry-level / small deployments

* Around **$499–$1,499 per month** (≈ £400–£1,200/month) for small user tiers ([itqlick.com][1])
* Usually limited to **100–500 active users**

---

### 📊 Standard business / mid-market

* Roughly **$30,000+ per year** starting price ([Zoftware][2])
* Common real-world range:
  👉 **$30,000 – $50,000+ per year** ([Beetsol LMS][3])

This is where most companies land.

---

### 📊 Enterprise / large scale

* Can go **$50k–$100k+ annually** depending on:

  * number of users
  * integrations (Salesforce, APIs, etc.)
  * customization and support ([Beetsol LMS][3])

---

## ⚙️ Extra costs (important)

* **Implementation/setup**:

  * ~$3k – $15k (SMB)
  * $20k+ for enterprise ([Software Finder][4])

* **Add-ons** (examples):

  * Data connectors: ~$25k
  * Language packs: ~$10k+ ([Software Finder][4])

---

## 🧠 How pricing actually works

Skilljar typically charges based on:

* **Annual subscription**
* **Active users (not total users)** ([Software Finder][4])

So if more customers use your training → your cost goes up.

---

## ⚠️ Straight talk

Skilljar is:

* **Not cheap**
* Positioned for **B2B SaaS / enterprise customer training**
* Often **overkill for small creators or solo courses**

---

## 👍 Quick rule of thumb

* Small team testing → **$5k–$15k total first year**
* Growing SaaS → **$30k–$50k/year**
* Enterprise → **$50k–$100k+/year**
