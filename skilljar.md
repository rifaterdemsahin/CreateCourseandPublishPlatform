# skilljar
**Skilljar** is a **cloud-based learning management system (LMS)** designed specifically for **customer education and training**.

Here’s a clear breakdown of what that means:

### 🧠 What Skilljar does

Skilljar helps companies **create and deliver online training programs** for:

* Customers
* Partners
* External users (not just employees)

Instead of traditional internal HR training systems, Skilljar focuses on **teaching people how to use a company’s product or service**. ([instruqt.com][1])

---

### 🚀 Key capabilities

* **Course creation & delivery** – Build online courses, videos, and learning paths
* **Customer onboarding** – Train new users quickly so they can start using a product effectively
* **Certifications** – Offer certificates or exams to validate knowledge
* **Monetization** – Sell courses or training subscriptions
* **Analytics** – Track engagement, completion, and impact on customer success ([Skilljar][2])

---

### 🎯 Why companies use it

Organizations use Skilljar to:

* Improve **product adoption** (customers actually learn how to use the product)
* Reduce **support costs** (fewer help tickets)
* Increase **customer retention and satisfaction**
* Scale training globally through self-service learning ([instruqt.com][1])

---

### 🧩 Simple way to think about it

* Traditional LMS → trains employees
* Skilljar → trains **customers and partners**
