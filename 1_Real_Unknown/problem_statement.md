# Problem Statement

## The Gap

Course creators and educators want to deliver structured learning experiences without paying enterprise LMS fees ($30k–$100k/year for platforms like Skilljar).

## The Opportunity

Build a **free, deployable proof-of-concept course platform** that:
- Hosts course content (YouTube embeds)
- Tracks student progress
- Provides an AI tutor for interactive learning
- Costs under $5/month to run

## Target Audience

- Solo course creators
- Small training teams
- Bootstrapped ed-tech startups

## Success Criteria

1. A student can browse modules, watch lessons, and mark progress
2. The AI tutor answers questions about course content
3. The entire platform deploys in one command
4. Monthly running cost stays under $5
